# Slow Cooker Stock

??? note "Ingredients"
    - Chicken bones (roasted) (4 from chicken thighs)
    - 1 onion, rough chopped
    - 2 celery stalks, rough chopped
    - 1 carrot, rough chopped
    - 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
    - 2 sprigs thyme
    - 1 bay leaf
    - 6-8 peppercorns

??? note "Method"
    1. Roast bones 200°C in toaster oven (20-25 mins)
    2. Add all ingredients to slow cooker
    3. Cover with cold water (2-3cm from rim)
    4. Set to LOW
    5. Cook 8-12 hours
    6. Strain through fine mesh strainer
    7. Cool quickly in ice bath
