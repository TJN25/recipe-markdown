
# Chicken Rice Bowl

**Serves:** 2

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Base"
        - 3/4 cup dry sushi rice

    ??? info "Protein"
        - 300 g simple marinade chicken

    ??? info "Vegetables / toppings"
        - 2–3 leaves iceberg lettuce, shredded
        - 1/3 carrot, grated
        - 2 inches cucumber, sliced or diced
        - 1 spring onion, finely sliced
        - 1/3 to 1/2 cup shelled edamame, for one bowl
        - 1/3 to 1/2 cup corn, for one bowl
        - 2–4 tbsp pickled onion
        - 1 capsicum, roasted and sliced

    ??? info "Sauces / finish"
        - 2–4 tbsp capsicum sauce
        - 1–2 tbsp kewpie
        - Sriracha, to taste
