# Croque **Monsieur**

- 50g [bechemel](recipes/bechemel.md) 
- 5g Parmegiano regiano
- 20g Colby cheese
- Slice of ham
- 2 Slices toast bread
- 1 tbsp Butter 
1. Heat the slice of ham in a hot pan
2. Butter both sides of the bread
3. In a hot pan toast both sides of the bread
4. Add béchamel, ham, cheese (grated), wrap and toast a little longer (cheese side down).
5. Rest a couple of minutes
6. Slice in two and enjoy
