# Mexican Spicy Sauce

**Makes:** approximately 500ml?

??? note "Ingredients"
    ??? info "Stage 1 (Roux Base)"
        - 2 tbsp oil (base, roux component)
        - 2 tbsp flour (thickener)22g
        - 2 chilis, diced (heat, flavor)
        - 3 cloves garlic, diced (aromatic base)
    ??? info "Stage 2 (Main Sauce)"
        - 2 cups water (liquid base)
        - 85g tomato paste (body, color)
        - 1 tsp cumin (earthy depth)2g
        - ½ tsp cayenne pepper (heat)2g
    ??? info "Stage 3 (Seasoning)"
        - ¾ tsp salt (seasoning)
        - Splash lemon juice (brightness)

??? note "Method"
    ??? info "Make Roux Base"
        - Combine oil, flour, chilis, and garlic in saucepan
        - Heat while whisking until mixture bubbles
        - Continue cooking for 1 minute, whisking constantly

    ??? info "Build Sauce"
        - Add water, tomato paste, cumin, and cayenne
        - Whisk to combine
        - Simmer until mixture begins to thicken

    ??? info "Season and Finish"
        - Add salt and lemon juice
        - Continue simmering until sauce coats back of spoon
        - Blend until smooth

??? note "Storage"
    - Store in airtight container
    - Keeps refrigerated for 1 week
    - Freeze portions for longer storage
    Note: Thickness can be adjusted with additional water if needed
