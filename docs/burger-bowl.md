
# Big Mac Cheeseburger Protein Bowl

**Serves:** 4

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "For the beef"

        * 500 g premium beef mince
        * 1 teaspoon salt
        * 1 teaspoon black pepper
        * 1 teaspoon garlic powder
        * 1 teaspoon onion powder

    ??? info "For the bowl"

        * 2/3 cups uncooked sushi rice
        * 4 cups shredded lettuce
        * 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
        * 1 cup dill pickles, sliced
        * 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
        * 1 cup cheese (110g), shredded

    ??? info "For the sauce"

        * 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
        * 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
        * 1 tablespoon tomato sauce or ketchup
        * 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
        * 1/2 teaspoon paprika
        * 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

??? note "Preparation"

    1. Cook the sushi rice if not already done.
    2. Shred the lettuce.
    3. Slice the tomatoes, pickles, and red onion.
    4. Grate the cheese if needed.
    5. Mix the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "For the beef"

        1. Heat a large frying pan over medium to medium-high heat.
        2. Add the mince and break it up as it cooks.
        3. Add the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
        4. Cook until browned and cooked through.
        5. Drain off excess fat if needed.

    ??? info "To assemble"

        1. Divide the cooked rice between 4 bowls.
        2. Top with the lettuce.
        3. Spoon over the hot beef.
        4. Add the cheddar so it softens slightly on the hot mince.
        5. Add the tomatoes, pickles, and red onion.
        6. Spoon or drizzle over the sauce.
