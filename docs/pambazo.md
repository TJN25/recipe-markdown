# Pambazo (Mexican Chorizo-Potato Sandwich)

**Serves:** 2

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Guajillo Chile Sauce"
        - 4-6 dried Kashmiri chilies (substitute for guajillo) - base heat/color
        - 1 chipotle
        - 2 cloves garlic - aromatic
        - 250g chicken broth (from your stock cubes) - liquid base
        - Salt to taste - seasoning

    ??? info "Filling"
        - 230g chorizo sausage - main protein/fat/flavor
        - 1 Agria potato (substitute for Yukon Gold), diced small - starch/bulk
        - Salt to taste

    ??? info "Assembly"
        - 4 Brioche buns
        - Shredded lettuce - fresh crunch
        - Feta cheese, crumbled (substitute for cotija) - salty/creamy
        - Parmesan, grated (substitute for cotija) - salty/umami
        - Pickled onions - acid/crunch
        - Smoky salsa verde - fresh/spicy element

??? note "Method"
    1. Guajillo Sauce (20 mins):
    - Toast Kashmiri chilies in cast iron pan (10-15 seconds per side)
    - Soak in hot water 10 minutes
    - Blend chilies, garlic, and broth in your standard blender
    - Strain through fine mesh strainer
    - Season with salt
    2. Chorizo-Potato Filling (15 mins):
    - Heat cast iron pan medium heat
    - Cook chorizo, breaking up (2-3 mins)
    - Add diced potato
    - Cook 8 minutes until potatoes soften
    3. Bread Prep:
    - Dip rolls in chile sauce to coat completely
    - Heat cast iron pan with canola oil
    - Sear bread both sides until toasted
    - Press gently with spatula while cooking
    4. Assembly:
    - Add chorizo-potato mixture to bottom half
    - Top with crumbled feta and grated parmesan
    - Add salsa verde
    - Add shredded lettuce
    - Finish with pickled onions
    - Close sandwich and slice

    ??? info "Pro Tips"
        - Par-boil potatoes for faster cooking
        - Make extra chile sauce - stores well
        - Use sturdy rolls that won't fall apart when soaked
        - Keep a clean tea towel handy - this can get messy!
