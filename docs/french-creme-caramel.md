# French Crème Caramel

**Serves:** 2

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Caramel"
        - 100g caster sugar
        - 30ml water

    ??? info "Custard"
        - 2 large eggs
        - 1 egg yolk
        - 250ml full-fat milk
        - 50g caster sugar
        - 1 tsp vanilla extract
        - Pinch of salt

??? note "Method"
    1. Make Caramel:
    - Combine sugar and water in small saucepan
    - Heat over medium heat without stirring until golden amber (8-10 minutes)
    - Immediately pour into 2 ramekins, swirl to coat bottom
    - Let cool completely until hard
    2. Make Custard:
    - Preheat toaster oven to 160°C
    - Heat milk until just below simmer
    - Whisk eggs, yolk, sugar and salt in bowl
    - Slowly pour hot milk into egg mixture while whisking
    - Add vanilla, strain through fine mesh
    3. Bake:
    - Pour custard into caramel-lined ramekins
    - Place in larger dish, add hot water halfway up sides
    - Bake 30-35 minutes until edges set but center jiggles
    - Cool at room temperature 30 minutes
    - Refrigerate minimum 4 hours
    4. Serve:
    - Let sit 10 minutes at room temperature
    - Run knife around edges
    - Place plate on top, flip quickly
    - Let caramel flow down sides

??? note "Notes"
    - Best made day before serving
    - Can be refrigerated up to 2 days
