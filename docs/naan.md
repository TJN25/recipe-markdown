
# Naan Bread

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 1⅛ cups all-purpose flour
    - ½ tsp sugar
    - ¼ cup warm water
    - 3.5g (½ sachet) active dry yeast
    - 1½ tbsp plain yogurt
    - ¼ tsp salt
    - ½ tbsp oil
    - Oil for greasing
    - 1½ tbsp melted butter
    - 1 clove garlic, crushed
    - Small pinch coriander, sliced thin

??? note "Method"

    1. Mix flour, sugar, water, yeast, salt, yogurt, and a drizzle of oil in a bowl.
    2. When dough forms a ball, turn out onto floured surface and knead 10 minutes until smooth.
    3. Place in greased bowl, cover with tea towel, and let rise 30 minutes until doubled.
    4. Turn dough onto oiled bench and cut into 4 pieces.
    5. Roll each piece into a tight ball, then roll out with rolling pin.
    6. Place garlic and coriander on one side, flip, and roll out again to embed.
    7. Cook naan on preheated cast iron pan in oven on fan bake at 215C for 3 minutes (only one side needed cooking to avoid over crisping). Brush with melted butter immediately after cooking.
