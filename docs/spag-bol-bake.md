# Spaghetti Bolognese Bake

**Serves:** 2

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "For the bolognese"

        - 1 medium onion, diced
        - 200g mushrooms, sliced
        - 1 tablespoon olive oil (can use vegetable oil or similar)
        - 2 cloves garlic, minced (or heaped tsp minced garlic)
        - 250g prime beef mince
        - 1 tablespoon lemon juice
        - 1 beef stock cube
        - 1/4 cup water
        - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
        - 1 fresh tomato, diced
        - 1/2 teaspoon salt
        - 1/2 teaspoon thyme
        - 1/2 teaspoon oregano
        - 250g spaghetti

    ??? info "For the cheese sauce"

        - 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
        - 3 tablespoons butter
        - 375ml trim milk
        - 1/2 teaspoon salt
        - 80g cheese, grated (I use colby)

    ??? info "For the topping"

        - 60g cheese, grated (I use colby, more cheese never hurts)
        - 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (I don't normally bother)

??? note "Preparation"

    1. Dice the onion.
    2. Slice the mushrooms.
    3. Mince the garlic.
    4. Dice the tomato.
    5. Grate the cheese and separate into sauce and topping portions.
    6. Chop the parsley.
    7. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil.

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "For the bolognese"

        1. Heat the oil in a large nonstick frying pan over medium-high heat.
        2. Add the onion and mushrooms. Cook for 10 to 15 minutes until softened and lightly browned. (can do shorter if in a hurry)
        3. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
        4. Add the mince, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes until browned.
        5. Add the lemon juice, crumbled stock cube, water, tomato paste, diced tomato, salt, thyme, and oregano.
        6. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes until thickened. (longer is better if there is time)

    ??? info "For the pasta"

        1. Add the spaghetti to boiling water and cook 1 to 2 minutes less than the package directions.
        2. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
        3. Drain and set aside.

    ??? info "For the cheese sauce"

        1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
        2. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1 minute. (until a wet sand consistency)
        3. Remove from the heat and gradually whisk in the milk. (making sure not to add too quickly or it'll be hard to remove the lumps)
        4. Return to the heat and whisk constantly for 5 to 6 minutes until thickened.
        5. Remove from the heat, add the salt and 80g grated cheese, and whisk until smooth.

    ??? info "To assemble"

        1. Preheat the toaster oven grill function.
        2. Layer the spaghetti in an oven-safe casserole dish.
        3. Spoon over the bolognese.
        4. Pour the cheese sauce evenly over the top.
        5. Sprinkle over the remaining 60g cheese.
        6. Grill for 4 to 5 minutes until golden and bubbling.
        7. Garnish with parsley.
