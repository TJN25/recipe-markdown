
# Homemade Kewpie-Style Mayo

    ??? info "Standard Batch (makes ~250ml/1 cup)"

        - **1 egg **(room temperature)
        - **2 tsp white vinegar (next time: add 1 tsp lemon juice)**
        - **1/4 tsp salt (next time: 3/8 tsp)**
        - **1/2 tsp sugar (next time: ¾ tsp)**
        - **1/4 tsp MSG** 
        - **200ml (3/4 cup + 2 tbsp) canola oil**

    ??? info "Method"

        1. **Blend base**: In a narrow container (ideal for immersion blender) combine egg, vinegar, salt, sugar, and MSG. Let sit 30 seconds.
        2. **Emulsify**:
           - *Immersion blender*: Pour in all the oil and let settle for 30 seconds. Place blender at bottom, turn on HIGH, and hold steady for 10-15 seconds until thick. Then gently move up and down to incorporate.
        3. **Adjust**: If too thick, add a few drops of water. Taste and adjust seasoning.

    ??? info "Notes"

        - This makes ~16 tbsp (homemade mayo keeps 3-5 days refrigerated)
        - Use the freshest eggs possible (or pasteurized if concerned about raw eggs)
        - Everything should be at room temperature for best emulsion
