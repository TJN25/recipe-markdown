# Chipotle Enchilada Sauce

**Makes:** enough for 5 small enchiladas (using small wraps)
**Serves:** 2-3 (for enchiladas)

??? note "Ingredients"

    * 1 1/2 tsp olive oil
    * 2 1/4 tsp flour
    * 125 ml water
    - 1 tsp chicken stock powder
    * 60 ml tomato sauce
    * 1 tbsp tomato paste
    * 1/2 can chipotles in adobo, chopped

    ??? info "Seasonings"

        * 1/8 tsp onion powder
        * 1/8 tsp garlic powder
        * 1/8 tsp salt
        * 1/2 tsp cumin
        * 1/2 tsp paprika
        * 1/2 tsp dried oregano
        * a few grinds of black pepper
        * 1/8 tsp cayenne

??? note "Method"

    1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Add the flour and stir for about 20 seconds.
    3. Add the spices and toast for 30 seconds (until fragrant smell)
    4. Add the chicken stock, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and chopped chipotles.
    5. Stir well, then cook, whisking regularly, until thickened.
