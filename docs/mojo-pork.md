
# Slow Cooker Cuban Mojo Pork

**Serves:** 3-4
**Prep:** 15 minutes (plus marinating) | **Cook:** 4-5 hours | **Total:** ~4h 15m-17h

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 750 g bone-in pork shoulder chops/pieces (~2 cm thick)
    - 5 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    - 1 big handful cilantro/coriander (roughly 1/2 cup loosely packed)
    - 1 tsp orange zest
    - 70 ml orange juice
    - 50 ml lime juice
    - 1 small pinch/handful mint leaves (about 1-2 tbsp loosely packed)
    - 3 garlic cloves
    - 1/2 tbsp fresh oregano (packed) OR 1 tsp dried oregano
    - 3/4 tsp ground cumin
    - 7.5 g salt
    - 3/8 tsp black pepper
    - 60-125 ml water, stock, or extra orange juice (for the slow cooker)

    ??? info "**Mojo sauce**"

        - 1 tbsp lime juice
        - 2 tbsp orange juice
        - Salt and pepper

??? note "Instructions"

    1. Blend (or finely chop and mix) the olive oil, cilantro, orange zest, orange juice, lime juice, mint, garlic, oregano, cumin, and black pepper.
    2. Cover pork with remaining salt and let rest for 20-60 minutes
    3. Put pork and marinade into a ziplock bag or container. Marinate in the fridge for 4-12 hours.
    4. Optional: brown pork in a hot pan with a little oil, 60-90 seconds per side. (forgot this, try next time)
    5. Add pork and all marinade to the slow cooker. Add 60-125 ml water/stock/orange juice. (did chicken stock, added too much water)
    6. Cook on LOW for 4-5 hours, checking from 3.5 hours, until very tender.
    7. Remove pork, pull meat off the bone, and shred/chunk. Rest 5-10 minutes, then toss with a little of the slow cooker juices to keep it juicy.

    ??? info "**Mojo sauce**"

        1. Strain **150–250 ml** slow cooker juices into a small saucepan (skim fat if you want).
        2. Add lime juice and orange juice. Simmer 2–5 minutes to taste. Season with salt and pepper.
