
??? note "Ingredients"
    - 125g beef mince (prime)
    - 15g red onion, pickle
    - 10g yellow mustard, mayo
    - 20g tomato sauce
    - pinch of smoked paprika
    - 2 slices cheese
    - burger bun
    - 1 leaf lettuce
    - salt and pepper

??? note "Method"
    1. Combine mustard, mayo, tomato sauce, paprika, and 10g onion and pickle (finely diced)
    2. Seperate mince into 2 even (very loose) balls
    3. Heat pan to as hot as is tolerable with the smoke and the surface (medium for me)
    4. Place meat on pan and smash down till very flat
    5. Add a pinch of salt and some pepper
    6. Cook for 1-2 minutes
    7. Flip, cook for another minute or so, adding cheese slices to the top of each
    8. Place slice bun in pan to lightly toast
    9. Place one patty on the other, and remove from heat (after the additional minute of cooking)
    10. Assemble, wrap, rest, enjoy
