
# Capsicum sauce

**Stage 1 (Base Prep)**
- Roasted red capsicum: 2 large (or ~300g if using jarred)
- Dried chillies: 4 chillies
- Hot water: ½ cup (enough to submerge the chillies)

**Stage 2 (Aromatics)**
- Garlic cloves: 4 cloves
- Tomato paste: 2 tbsp (eyeballed)
- Sumac: 1 tsp
- Marjoram: 1 tsp
- Cumin 1 tsp
- Coriander powder: 1 tsp
- Sweet paprika: 1 tsp
- Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
- Rice vinegar: 1 tbsp (more to adjust)
- Soy sauce: 1 tbsp
- Honey: 1 tbsp (Eyeballed)
- Salt: to taste (a pinch was correct) 

Toast spices 

Roast capsicum 16 minutes @230C and garlic 8 minutes. 

Blend. Adjust consistency with water (1 tbsp was enough) from soaked chillies.
