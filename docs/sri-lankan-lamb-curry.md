# Sri Lankan Spicy Lamb Curry (2 servings)

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 400g lamb, cut into large bite-sized pieces (protein, main component)
    - 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (heat, adjust to taste)
    - 2 pinches (~1.5g) salt (seasoning, flavor enhancer)
    - 1 tbsp soy sauce (umami, color)
    - 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped (aromatic base, flavor)
    - 1 tbsp olive oil (cooking medium, flavor carrier)
    - 1/2 tsp mustard powder (pungent heat, replaces seeds)
    - 1/2 tsp fenugreek leaves, crushed (unique bitter-sweet note)
    - 2 black cardamom pods, bruised (aromatic, depth)
    - 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder (warm spice note, easier to distribute)
    - 1 tsp ground coriander (earthy flavor)
    - 1 tsp ground cumin (warm, earthy notes)
    - 1 bay leaf (depth, background aromatics)
    - 1 kashmiri chilli (heat)
    - 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped (base flavor, texture)
    - 1 carrot, diced
    - 1/2 cup coconut milk (richness, sauce body, traditional fat) (maybe more)

??? note "Method"
    ??? info "Prep Phase (30 minutes):"
        1. Marinate lamb with chili powder, salt, soy sauce, and garlic
        2. Crush fenugreek leaves and cardamom pods with pestle and mortar
    ??? info "Cooking Phase (45 minutes):"
        1. Heat oil in cast iron pan over medium heat
        1. Sear meat
        1. Add mustard powder, fenugreek, cardamom, cinnamon, carrot, bay leaf and ground spices
        1. Reduce heat to low, cover, cook 25 minutes
        1. Add onions, cook 15 minutes
        1. Add coconut milk, simmer 5 minutes
        1. Add lime juice to to taste

??? note "Notes"
    - Coconut milk is the traditional fat base (authentic flavor)
    - Remove bay leaf before serving
    Much better using your pantry!
