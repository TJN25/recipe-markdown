# Mexican-Style Cottage Pie

**Serves:** 4-6

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 500g mince *(protein base)*
    - 1 large onion, diced *(sweetness, moisture)*
    - 1 carrot, diced *(sweetness, texture)*
    - 2-3 chipotle peppers from can, finely chopped *(smoky heat)*
    - 2 tbsp chipotle adobo sauce *(concentrated smoky flavor)*
    - 800g potatoes, peeled and cubed *(creamy topping)*
    - 1 bay leaf *(aromatic depth)*
    - 2 tsp ground cumin *(earthy warmth)*
    - 1 tsp smoked paprika *(color, mild smoke)*
    - 1 tsp oregano *(herbaceous)*
    - 2 tbsp tomato paste *(umami, thickening)*
    - 1 stock cube + 400ml water *(moisture, flavor)*
    - 1 tsp red wine vinegar *(acid balance)*
    - ¼ tsp MSG *(umami boost)*
    - 2 tbsp olive oil *(fat for cooking)*
    - 50g butter *(richness for potatoes)*
    - 50ml milk *(creaminess)*
    - 100g cheese, grated *(melting richness)*
    - 20g parmesan, finely grated *(salty topping)*
    - Salt and pepper *(seasoning)*

??? note "Preparation"

    ??? info "Prep Phase"
        1. Preheat oven to 200°C fan forced. Dice onion and carrot to 1cm pieces. Finely chop chipotles. Cube potatoes evenly.

    ??? info "Steam Potatoes"
        Place potatoes in steamer basket over boiling water in large saucepan. Cover and steam 20-25 minutes until tender.

    ??? info "Mince Base"
        Heat olive oil in large fry pan over medium-high heat. Add onion and carrot, cook 5 minutes until softening. Add mince, breaking up with wooden spoon. Cook until browned, about 8 minutes.

    ??? info "Drain Fat"
        Tip mince into colander over bowl, drain 2-3 minutes. Reserve rendered fat. Return mince to pan.

    ??? info "Spice and Sauce"
        Add cumin, paprika, oregano, bay leaf - cook 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in tomato paste, cook 2 minutes. Add chipotles, adobo sauce, stock cube dissolved in water, red wine vinegar, and MSG. Simmer 10 minutes until thickened. Remove bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper.

    ??? info "Mash Potatoes"
        Mash potatoes with butter, milk, reserved rendered fat, and half the grated cheese until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.

    ??? info "Assemble"
        Transfer mince mixture to large casserole dish. Top with mashed potatoes, spreading evenly. Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese and parmesan. Create texture on surface using fork.

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Bake"
        Cook 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbling. Rest 5 minutes before serving.

??? note "Notes"

    ??? info "Enhancement Suggestions"
        - Add diced bell peppers with onion for extra sweetness
        - Include black beans or corn kernels for texture
        - Mix fresh coriander into mashed potatoes
        - Top with sliced jalapeños before baking

    ??? info "Mince Options"
        - Beef mince for rich, classic flavor
        - Lamb mince for deeper, gamier taste
        - Pork mince for sweetness and fat content
        - Turkey mince for leaner option
        - Mix of beef and pork for balanced flavor

    ??? info "Cheese Alternatives"
        - Cheddar for sharp flavor
        - Gruyere for nutty richness
        - Manchego for Spanish twist
        - Aged gouda for caramel notes

    ??? info "Storage Advice"
        - Freeze individual portions in ramekins
        - Store leftovers refrigerated up to 4 days
        - Reheat in toaster oven at 180°C for 15 minutes

    ??? info "Notes on Techniques, Ingredients, and Plating"
        - Steam potatoes with lid slightly ajar for last 5 minutes to reduce moisture
        - The reserved fat creates restaurant-quality creamy potatoes
        - MSG amplifies all umami flavors - don't skip it
        - Pipe mashed potatoes for elegant presentation
        - Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with lime wedges and sour cream
