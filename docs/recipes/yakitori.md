??? note "YAKITORI (4–6 skewers)"

    ??? info "Ingredients"

        **Yakitori Tare (glaze):**
        - 2 tbsp mirin *(sweet, helps glaze stick)*
        - 2 tbsp soy sauce *(salty, umami)*
        - 2 tbsp brown sugar *(sweet, caramelises)*
        - 2 tbsp water *(replaces sake; controls reduction)*

        **Skewers:**
        - 120 g chicken thigh *(fat, juicy)*
        - 1 spring onion, white and light green parts *(sweet when grilled)*
        - 4–6 bamboo or metal skewers *(if bamboo, soak 20 minutes to prevent burning)*

    ??? info "Preparation"

        **Prep the glaze (can be done hours ahead):**
        1. Combine mirin, soy sauce, brown sugar, and water in a small heat-proof bowl or very small saucepan.
        2. Stir to dissolve sugar slightly.
        3. **Stovetop method:** Simmer over low heat for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until glossy and slightly thickened (should coat the back of a spoon). Remove from heat.
           **Alternative (if you must use microwave):** Cover bowl loosely with a plate, microwave on high 2–3 minutes, pausing to stir every minute. Watch carefully to avoid boiling over.
        4. Set aside to cool. Glaze thickens further as it cools.
