# Chicken Tikka Masala

**Serves:** 2-3

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Proteins & Dairy"
        - 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs
        - 60ml Greek yogurt
        - 45ml heavy cream
        - 15g butter

    ??? info "Fresh Ingredients"
        - 1 medium onion
        - 5cm fresh ginger (divided use)
        - 7 garlic cloves (divided use)
        - 1 lemon (for 15ml juice)

    ??? info "Pantry Items"
        - 15g cashews
        - 400g can whole tomatoes
        - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
        - 30ml canola oil
        - 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
        - 2 tablespoons tandoori paste

    ??? info "Spices"
        - 2 dried chilies
        - 1 black cardamom pods (or 3 green)
        - 1 cloves
        - 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
        - 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
        - 3 teaspoons garam masala (divided use)
        - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
        - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
        - Salt to taste

??? note "Timeline"

    ??? info "Up to 24 hours ahead"

    ??? info "Marinate Chicken"
        - 500g chicken thighs, cut into 2.5cm pieces
        - Mix marinade:
        - 60ml Greek yogurt
        - 2.5cm ginger, grated
        - 3 garlic cloves, minced
        - 1 teaspoon kosher salt
        - 2 teaspoons garam masala
        - 15ml lemon juice
        - 2 tablespoons tandoori paste?
        - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
        - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

    ??? info "1 hour 15 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Prep for Sauce"
        - Toast and grind spices
        - Soak cashews in 60ml hot water
        - Dice onion
        - Prepare remaining ginger and garlic

    ??? info "1 hour ahead"

    ??? info "Make Sauce"
        - 30ml canola oil
        - Onion with baking soda
        - Remaining garlic and ginger
        - Ground spices
        - Tomato paste
        - Soaked cashews with water
        - Tomatoes
        - 100ml water
        - Salt to taste

    ??? info "15 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Cook Chicken"
        - Broil until charred and 75°C internal

    ??? info "5 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Finish Sauce"
        - Blend until smooth
        - Add cream and butter
        - Add final teaspoon garam masala
        - Fold in chicken

    ??? info "Marination"
        1. Mix all marinade ingredients in bowl
        2. Coat chicken thoroughly
        3. Refrigerate in covered container

    ??? info "Sauce"
        1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high
        2. Cook onions with baking soda until deep brown (14-17 minutes)
        3. Add ginger and garlic, cook 1 minute
        4. Add spices, stir 30 seconds
        5. Add tomato paste, cook 1 minute
        6. Add cashews, tomatoes, water
        7. Simmer 40 minutes until reduced and deepened in color

??? note "Final Assembly"
    1. Blend sauce until smooth
    2. Add butter and cream
    3. Stir in final garam masala
    4. Fold in broiled chicken
    5. Adjust seasoning

??? note "Notes"

    ??? info "Heat Level"
        - Medium to hot
        - Adjust with cayenne and dried chili quantity

??? note "Storage"
    - 1 week refrigerated
    - Freeze sauce without cream (3 months)

??? note "Tips"
    - More charring on chicken is desirable for tikka masala
    - Sauce should be deeper red and more intensely spiced than butter chicken
    - If sauce is too thick after blending, add hot water gradually
    - Final garam masala addition is crucial for authentic taste

    ??? info "Key Differences from Butter Chicken"
        - More garlic and ginger
        - Addition of tandoori paste
        - Less cream and butter
        - More complex spicing
        - Deeper red color
        - Higher heat level
