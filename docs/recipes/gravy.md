# Gravy

**Serves:** 2–3

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1/2 chicken stock cube / bouillon cube
    - 1/2 beef stock cube / bouillon cube
    - 280 ml boiling water
    - 2 tbsp butter, unsalted
    - 2 tbsp flour, plain / all purpose
    - 1/4 tsp onion powder
    - 1/8 tsp finely ground black pepper
    - Salt, if needed

??? note "Method"
    1. Crumble cubes into boiling water, mix to dissolve.
    2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
    3. Add flour, onion powder and pepper. Mix into butter with a wooden spoon or whisk.
    4. While stirring, slowly pour in half the liquid. Once incorporated into the flour butter roux, add remaining liquid and mix.
    5. Stir for 1–2 minutes until it thickens to a gravy consistency. Taste, add more salt and pepper if needed.
    6. Serve over chops, steak, sausages, veggies, roast chicken, pork, beef or lamb.
