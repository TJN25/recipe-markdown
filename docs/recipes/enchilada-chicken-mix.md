# Enchilada Chicken Filling

**Makes:** enough for 5 enchiladas (using small wraps)
**Serves:** 2-3 (for enchiladas)

??? note "Ingredients"

    * 300 g chicken, diced
    * 1 tbsp olive oil, divided
    * 1 small onion, sliced
    * 1 garlic clove, finely minced
    * 1 red capsicum sliced
    * 100 g refried beans (optional)
    * 100 g frozen corn
    * 2 tbsp water
    * 1 tsp onion powder
    * 1 tsp garlic powder
    * 1 tsp salt (3-4g)
    * 2 tsp cumin
    * 2 tsp paprika
    * 2 tsp dried oregano
    * 1/4 tsp black pepper
    * cayenne, as wanted

??? note "Method"

    1. Combine the chicken with 1/2 tbsp olive oil and all seasonings. Leave it to marinade for up to 24 hours.
    2. Heat the remaining 1/2 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat (hotter if outdoors/the range can handle it).
    3. Add the chicken and cook for about 2 minutes, then continue as needed until cooked the way you want.
    4. Add the onion, capsicum, and garlic to the same pan and cook for 2 minutes.
    5. Add the refried beans, corn, water, and cooked chicken.
    6. Stir and cook for about 2 minutes until the filling is thick and juicy.
