# Classic Baked Ziti

**Serves:** 1-2

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 200g ziti pasta *(tube-shaped pasta that holds sauce)*
    - 200g Italian-style pork sausages or chorizo *(protein, flavor) (alternate, use 300g chicken breast)*
    - 2 tbsp tomato paste *(concentrated base)*
    - 400g canned diced tomatoes *(liquid and chunks)*
    - 250g ricotta cheese *(creamy layer)*
    - 150g mozzarella cheese, shredded *(melting cheese)*
    - 50g parmesan cheese, grated *(sharp flavor, browning)*
    - 1 egg *(binds ricotta mixture)*
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced *(aromatic base)*
    - 1 tsp dried basil *(herb flavor)*
    - 1 tsp dried oregano *(Italian seasoning)*
    - Salt, pepper, pinch of sugar *(seasoning)*
    - 3 tbsp olive oil *(fat for cooking)*
    - 1 small onion, diced *(optional - sweetness)*

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Prep (10 minutes)"
        1. Preheat oven to 190°C

        Bring large pot of salted water to boil

        Remove sausage from casings, crumble into chunks

        Mix ricotta, egg, half the mozzarella, half the parmesan, basil, oregano, salt and pepper in bowl

    ??? info "Cook Pasta (10 minutes)"
        1. Cook ziti according to package directions until al dente

        Drain, set aside

    ??? info "Make Sauce (15 minutes)"
        1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in large pan over medium heat

        Brown sausage chunks, breaking up with spoon, 5-6 minutes

        Add onion (if using), cook 3 minutes until soft

        Add garlic, cook 30 seconds until fragrant

        Add tomato paste, cook 1 minute, stirring constantly

        Add canned tomatoes, remaining oregano and basil

        Season with salt, pepper, pinch of sugar

        Simmer 10 minutes until slightly thickened

    ??? info "Assembly (5 minutes)"
        1. Mix cooked pasta with sauce in large bowl

        Layer half pasta mixture in baking dish

        Spread ricotta mixture evenly over pasta

        Top with remaining pasta mixture

        Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and parmesan on top

        Drizzle with remaining 1 tbsp olive oil

    ??? info "Bake"
        - 25-30 minutes until cheese is melted and golden brown

        Let rest 10 minutes before serving

??? note "Storage"

    Refrigerate leftovers up to 4 days, reheat in oven until heated through.
