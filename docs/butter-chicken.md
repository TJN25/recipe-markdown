
# Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

**Serves:** 2-3

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Proteins & Dairy"

        - 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs
        - 30ml Greek yogurt
        - 60ml heavy cream
        - 30g butter

    ??? info "Fresh Ingredients"

        - 1/2 medium onion
        - 4cm fresh ginger (divided use)
        - 2 garlic cloves

    ??? info "Pantry Items"

        - 15g cashews
        - 400g can whole tomatoes
        - 30ml canola oil
        - 1/8 teaspoon baking soda

    ??? info "Spices"

        - 4 teaspoons fenugreek leaves (divided use)
        - 3 teaspoons garam masala (divided use)
        - 1 dried chili
        - 1 black cardamom pod (or 2 green)
        - 1 clove
        - 1.5 teaspoon kosher salt (plus more to taste)

??? note "Timeline"

    ??? info "Up to 24 hours ahead"

    ??? info "Marinate Chicken"

        - 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 2.5cm pieces

        - Base marinade:

        - 30ml Greek yogurt
        - 2cm fresh ginger, grated (1/2 tsp ginger powder)
        - 1.5 teaspoon kosher salt
        - 1.5 teaspoons garam masala

        - Additional marinade ingredients:

        - 1.5 teaspoons fenugreek leaves, toasted and ground

    ??? info "1 hour 15 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Prep for Sauce"

        - Toast and grind:

        - 2.5 teaspoons fenugreek leaves
        - 1 dried chili
        - 1 black cardamom pod (or 2 green) one of each was great
        - 1 cloves
        - 1.5 teaspoons garam masala

        - Soak 15g cashews crushed (can soak in 60ml hot water)
        - Dice 1/2 medium onion
        - Prepare 2cm ginger and 2 garlic cloves (1/2 tsp ginger, teaspoon of minced garlic)

    ??? info "1 hour ahead"

    ??? info "Make Sauce"

        - 30ml canola oil
        - Prepared onion with 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
        - Prepared garlic and ginger
        - Ground spice mixture
        - Soaked cashews with water
        - 400g can whole tomatoes
        - 150ml water
        - Salt to taste

    ??? info "15 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Cook Chicken"

        - Preheat toaster oven on high broil
        - Broil marinated chicken 12-15 minutes until 75°C internal

    ??? info "5 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Finish Sauce"

        - Blend sauce until smooth
        - 60ml heavy cream
        - 30g butter
        - Fold in cooked chicken

??? note "To Serve"

    - Garnish with cream drizzle
    - Basmati rice (start 20 minutes ahead, 60g dry per person)

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Marination"

        1. Toast fenugreek in cast iron pan until fragrant (30 seconds)
        2. Mix all marinade ingredients in bowl
        3. Coat chicken thoroughly
        4. Refrigerate in covered container

    ??? info "Sauce"

        1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high
        2. Cook onions with baking soda until deep brown (14-17 minutes)
        3. Add ginger and garlic, cook 1 minute
        4. Add spices, stir 30 seconds
        5. Add cashews, tomatoes, water
        6. Simmer 40 minutes until reduced

    ??? info "Final Assembly"

        1. Blend sauce until smooth
        2. Add butter and cream
        3. Fold in broiled chicken
        4. Adjust seasoning

??? note "Notes"

    ??? info "Heat Level"

        - Mild to medium
        - Adjust with dried chili quantity

    ??? info "Storage"

        - 1 week refrigerated
        - Freeze sauce without cream (3 months)

    ??? info "Tips"

        - Keep sauce at gentle simmer
        - Check chicken frequently while broiling
        - For extra richness, add 1 tablespoon butter at end
