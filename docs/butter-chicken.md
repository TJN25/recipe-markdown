
# Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

**Serves:** 2-3

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Marinade"

        * 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 2.5cm pieces
        * 30ml Greek yogurt
        * 2cm fresh ginger, grated (1/2 tsp ginger powder)
        * 1.5 teaspoon kosher salt
        * 1.5 teaspoons garam masala
        * 1.5 teaspoons fenugreek leaves, toasted and ground

    ??? info "Sauce"

        * 30ml canola oil
        * 1/2 medium onion, diced
        * 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
        * 2cm fresh ginger
        * 2 garlic cloves
        * 2.5 teaspoons fenugreek leaves
        * 1 dried chili
        * 1 black cardamom pod (or 2 green) one of each was great
        * 1 clove
        * 1.5 teaspoons garam masala
        * 15g cashews, crushed
        * 60ml hot water (for soaking cashews)
        * 400g can whole tomatoes
        * 150ml water
        * Salt to taste

    ??? info "Finish"

        * 60ml heavy cream
        * 30g butter
        * Cooked chicken

    ??? info "To serve"

        * Cream drizzle
        * Basmati rice (start 20 minutes ahead, 60g dry per person)

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Marinade"

        1. Toast fenugreek in cast iron pan until fragrant (30 seconds).
        2. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
        3. Coat chicken thoroughly.
        4. Refrigerate in a covered container for up to 24 hours.

    ??? info "Preparation"

        1. Toast and grind the fenugreek leaves, dried chili, cardamom pod, clove, and garam masala.
        2. Soak the crushed cashews in 60ml hot water.
        3. Dice the onion.
        4. Prepare the ginger and garlic.

    ??? info "Make sauce"

        1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium-high.
        2. Cook the onion with the baking soda until deep brown.
        3. Add the ginger and garlic and cook for 1 minute.
        4. Add the ground spice mixture and stir for 30 seconds.
        5. Add the cashews with their soaking water, the tomatoes, 150ml water, and salt to taste.
        6. Simmer for 40 minutes until reduced.

    ??? info "Cook chicken"

        1. Preheat the toaster oven on high grill.
        2. Grill the marinated chicken for 12-15 minutes, until 75°C internal.
        3. Chop if needed and set aside.

    ??? info "Finish sauce"

        1. Blend the sauce until smooth.
        2. Add the cream and butter.
        3. Fold in the cooked chicken.
        4. Adjust seasoning.
