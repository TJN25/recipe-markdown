# BBQ Sauce Recipe

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 1 cup Ketchup
    - 1/3 cup Apple cider vinegar
    - 2 tbsp Kecap manis
    - 2-3 tbsp Brown sugar
    - 1/2 tsp MSG
    - 1 tsp Smoked paprika
    - 1 tsp Garlic powder
    - 1/2 tsp Black pepper

??? note "Method"

    1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan
    2. Heat on medium-low, stirring until sugar dissolves
    3. Simmer for 8-10 minutes until thickened
    4. Taste and adjust seasoning with soy sauce if needed
