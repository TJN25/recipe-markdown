# Spiced Lamb Pita with Sauces

2 generous servings
Core Components
Spiced Lamb
- 2-3 lamb & thyme sausages, meat removed from casings
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- Salt and pepper to taste

??? note "Method"
    1. Remove sausage meat from casings
    2. Mix all spices into meat thoroughly
    3. Heat large pan over medium-high heat
    4. Cook lamb in small pieces, breaking up while cooking
    5. Continue until crispy but still juicy
    6. Drain excess fat if needed
    Spicy Ketchup Sauce
    - 1/2 cup homemade ketchup
    - 1 tsp hot chili powder
    - 1/2 tsp ground cumin
    - 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
    - Cayenne to taste

??? note "Method"
    1. Mix all ingredients in small bowl
    2. Adjust heat level to taste
    3. Let stand 10 minutes for flavors to meld
    Assembly

    ??? info "Layer in warm pita pockets"
        1. Garlic hummus (separate recipe)
        2. Seasoned couscous (separate recipe)
        3. Crispy spiced lamb
        4. Spicy ketchup sauce
        5. Mint yogurt sauce (separate recipe)
        6. Shredded iceberg lettuce
        7. Baby spinach leaves
        8. Thinly sliced red onion
        Serve immediately while pita is warm
