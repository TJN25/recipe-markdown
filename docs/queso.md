
# Cottage Cheese Queso

**Serves:** about 4
**Prep:** 10 minutes
**Cook:** 5–10 minutes

??? note "Ingredients"

    * 2 oz medium cheddar, freshly grated
    * 4 oz colby, freshly grated
    * 1 x 454 g tub low-fat cottage cheese
    * 1–2 tsp finely grated parmigiano
    * 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
    * 1/4 tsp chili powder
    * 1/4 tsp ground cumin
    * 1/2 tsp onion powder
    * 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    * Salt, to taste

??? note "Method"

    1. Blend the cottage cheese until completely smooth.
    2. Pour it into a small saucepan and heat over low, stirring regularly so it does not catch on the bottom.
    3. Once warm, add the cheddar and colby. Stir until fully melted and smooth.
    4. Add the parmigiano, smoked paprika, chili powder, cumin, onion powder, and garlic powder.
    5. Stir until combined, then taste and add salt as needed.
    6. Serve warm.
