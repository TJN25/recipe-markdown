# Chicken Korma

UNVERIFIED DO NOT FOLLOW WITHOUT COMPARING TO BUTTER CHICKEN RECIPE
**Serves:** 2-3

??? note "Timeline"

    ??? info "Up to 24 hours ahead"

    ??? info "Marinate Chicken"
        - 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 2.5cm pieces
        - Base marinade:
        - 30ml Greek yogurt
        - 2cm fresh ginger, grated
        - 2 garlic cloves, minced
        - 1.5 teaspoon kosher salt
        - 1.5 teaspoons garam masala
        - Additional marinade ingredients:
        - 15g ground almonds
        - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
        - 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
        - 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

    ??? info "1 hour 15 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Prep for Sauce"
        - Toast and grind:
        - 4 green cardamom pods
        - 2 cloves
        - ½ tsp cinnamon
        - Soak separately:
        - 30g cashews in 60ml hot water
        - 15g ground almonds in 60ml hot water
        - Dice 1/2 medium onion
        - Prepare 2cm ginger and 4 garlic cloves

    ??? info "1 hour ahead"

    ??? info "Make Sauce"
        - 30ml canola oil
        - Prepared onion with 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
        - Prepared garlic and ginger
        - Ground spice mixture
        - Both nut mixtures with their soaking water
        - 200ml water
        - 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
        - Salt to taste

    ??? info "15 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Cook Chicken"
        - Heat large pan over medium-high OR oven convection setting 180C for 12-15 minutes (check temp reaches 75C internal)
        - Cook marinated chicken in batches until just done (no broiling)

    ??? info "5 minutes ahead"

    ??? info "Finish Sauce"
        - Blend sauce until very smooth
        - 90ml heavy cream?
        - 30g butter?
        - Fold in cooked chicken
        - 1 tablespoon (7g) ground almonds

??? note "To Serve"
    - Garnish with:
    - Flaked almonds
    - Fresh coriander
    - Basmati rice (start 20 minutes ahead, 60g dry per person)

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Marination"
        1. Mix all marinade ingredients in bowl
        2. Coat chicken thoroughly
        3. Refrigerate in covered container

    ??? info "Sauce"
        1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high
        2. Cook onions with baking soda until light golden (12-15 minutes)
        3. Add ginger and garlic, cook 1 minute
        4. Add spices, stir 30 seconds
        5. Add nut mixtures and water
        6. Simmer 30 minutes until thickened

??? note "Final Assembly"
    1. Blend sauce until very smooth
    2. Add butter and cream
    3. Fold in cooked chicken
    4. Stir in final ground almonds
    5. Adjust seasoning

??? note "Notes"

    ??? info "Heat Level"
        - Mild
        - Black pepper provides gentle warmth

??? note "Storage"
    - 5 days refrigerated
    - Not recommended for freezing due to high cream content

??? note "Tips"
    - Sauce should be pale golden, never brown
    - Blend sauce extra long for silky smoothness
    - If too thick after blending, add warm cream
    - Can substitute coconut cream for heavy cream
    - For extra richness, add ground cashews at the end
    - Don't brown the onions as deeply as other curries

    ??? info "Key differences from other curries"
        - No tomatoes
        - Higher nut content
        - Gentler cooking process
        - Paler color
        - More cream
        - No charring on chicken
