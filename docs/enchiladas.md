
# Chicken Enchiladas

**Serves:** 2
**Prep:** 20 minutes
**Cook:** 45 minutes

??? note "Ingredients"

    * 2 tortillas, flour or corn

    ??? info "Enchilada Seasoning"

        * 1/4 tsp onion powder
        * 1/4 tsp garlic powder
        * 1/4 tsp salt
        * 3/4 tsp cumin powder
        * 3/4 tsp paprika
        * 3/4 tsp dried oregano
        * pinch black pepper
        * scant 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, optional

    ??? info "Enchilada Sauce"

        * 1 1/2 tsp olive oil
        * 2 1/4 tsp flour
        * 60 ml tomato sauce
        * 1 tbsp tomato paste
        * 125 ml chicken stock / broth
        * 1/2 can chipotles in adobo, chopped

    ??? info "Chicken Filling"

        * 150 g chicken breast, diced
        * 1 1/2 tsp olive oil, separated
        * 2 tbsp onion, chopped
        * 1 small garlic clove, finely minced
        * 1/4 red capsicum / bell pepper, diced
        * 100 g refried beans
        * 100 g canned corn, drained
        * 1 tbsp water
        * 25 g cheese, shredded

    ??? info "Topping"

        * 40 g cheese, shredded
        * 1 1/2 tsp coriander / cilantro, roughly chopped

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Enchilada Seasoning"

        1. Mix all seasoning ingredients together.

    ??? info "Enchilada Sauce"

        1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
        2. Add the flour and stir for about 20 seconds.
        3. Add **1 1/2 tsp** of the enchilada seasoning, the tomato sauce, tomato paste, chicken stock, and the chopped chipotles in adobo.
        4. Stir well and cook, whisking regularly, until the sauce thickens.
        5. Remove from the heat.

    ??? info "Chicken Filling"

        1. Toss the diced chicken with **3/4 tsp** olive oil and the remaining seasoning.
        2. Heat the remaining **3/4 tsp** oil in a skillet.
        3. Cook the chicken until done, then set aside.
        4. In the same pan, cook the onion and garlic for 1 minute.
        5. Add the capsicum and cook for about 2 minutes until softened.
        6. Add the refried beans, cooked chicken, corn, and water.
        7. Stir and cook for about 2 minutes until the filling is thick and juicy.

    ??? info "Assemble and Bake"

        1. Preheat the oven to **180°C**.
        2. Lay out the tortillas.
        3. Divide the filling between them, placing it on the lower third.
        4. Sprinkle with the filling cheese, then roll up seam-side down.
        5. Smear a little sauce over the base of a small baking dish.
        6. Add the enchiladas, pour over the remaining sauce, then top with the shredded cheese.
        7. Bake for **20 to 25 minutes** until the cheese is melted and golden.
        8. Serve with coriander on top.
