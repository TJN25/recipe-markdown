# Spiced Couscous

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1/2 cup couscous (starch base, texture)
    - 1/2 cup hot chicken stock (flavor, cooking medium)
    - 1/2 teaspoon cumin (warmth, earthiness)
    - 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 
    - 1/2 teaspoon sumac
    - 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (sweet warmth)
    - 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika (depth, subtle smokiness)
    - Pinch of cayenne pepper (heat, optional)
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil (richness, prevents sticking)
    - 1 tablespoon lemon juice (brightness, acidity)
    - 2 tablespoons cashews, toasted and chopped (crunch)
    - Salt and pepper to taste (seasoning)
     - ¼ tsp of salt was used

??? note "Method"
    1. Place couscous in bowl
    1. Add toasted spices, salt, and pepper
    1. Pour hot stock over mixture, stir quickly
    1. Cover with a plate and let stand for 5 minutes
    1. Fluff couscous with a fork
    1. Stir in olive oil, lemon juice, most of the toasted cashews, and mint
    1. Season to taste
