# Salsa Verde

(Makes ~150ml - perfect for 4 servings)

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1 regular fresh tomato (base, freshness)
    - Lime juice to taste
    - 1 bird's eye chili (heat, can adjust to taste)
    - 2 cloves garlic (savory depth)
    - 1/4 white onion (texture, allium base)
    - Salt to taste (flavor enhancer)
    - 1 tablespoon Spanish olive oil (smoothness)
    - 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (depth)
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (herbal notes)

??? note "Preparation Steps"
    1. Salsa Verde (Make First):
    - Finely dice tomato, onion, chili, and garlic
    - Mix with lime juice, let sit 5 minutes
    - Add oil and spices
    - Blend briefly with stick blender
    - Season to taste
    - Let rest while preparing sandwich
