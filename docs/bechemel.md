
# Bechemel

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1/2 onion, diced
    - 15g butter
    - 15g flour
    - 150g milk
    - Salt
    - Pepper

??? note "Method"
    1. Melt butter over medium low heat
    2. Mix in flour and cook until sandy consistency
    3. Add room temperature milk a little at a time, stirring until smooth before adding more
    4. Add a pinch of salt and cracked black pepper to taste (subtle is *waaaay* better than over done)
