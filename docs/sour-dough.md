
# Sour Dough

To make bread 

- I put 162g water, 75g starter and 250g flour

 - mix then add 5g salt 

Then leave about 10 min and fold (will send you a video), leave  30 min then fold again until you have done that 4 times… then leave 2 hours and turn out onto bench (I put a bit of rice flour on the bench) and fold it into thirds, you don’t need to be specific and put into a proofing bowl and cover with tea towel and put in fridge overnight… then take out in the morning and allow to warm a little at room temp. I cook mine in a cast iron pot with the lid on at 250C fan bake. I put the pot in the oven to heat up to temp then when it is then tip the bread in, score the top with a knife and cook for 30 min.
