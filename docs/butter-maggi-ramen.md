# Simple Butter & Maggi Ramen

A quick, flavorful ramen with buttery richness, umami depth, and fresh spring onion.

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1 packet ramen noodles (NZ-style)
    - 1 spring onion, whites and greens separated
    - 2 eggs (1 scrambled, 1 poached)
    - 1 tbsp butter
    - 1-2 tsp Maggi seasoning sauce (to taste)
    - Salt & pepper, to taste
    - 1/4 tsp American mustard (optional)

??? note "Method"
    1. Cook ramen in minimal water for 3-4 minutes, slightly undercooking. Reserve some noodle water, then drain.
    2. In a frying pan, sauté the white parts of the spring onion over medium heat. Crack in one egg, scramble gently, and mix with the onion.
    3. Add a splash of reserved noodle water to deglaze the pan, then stir in the cooked noodles and butter.
    4. Season with Maggi seasoning sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. If using, mix in a small amount of mustard for extra depth.
    5. Serve topped with the greens of the spring onion and a poached egg.

??? note "Notes"
    - For a richer dish, cook the scrambled egg gently so it stays soft.
    - Adjust Maggi seasoning to taste, as it’s quite salty.
    - Mustard adds a subtle tang but is completely optional.
