# Agria Potato Wedges

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 2 medium Agria potatoes (starchy, fluffy interior, crisp exterior)
    - 2 tbsp avocado oil (high heat tolerance, neutral flavor)
    - 1 tsp smoked paprika (smoky flavor, color)
    - 1/2 tsp garlic powder (aromatic, flavor)
    - 1/2 tsp salt (seasoning)
    - 1/4 tsp black pepper (heat, seasoning)
    - 1 tsp mixed herbs (aromatic, flavor complexity)

    ??? info "Prep"
        1. Preheat air fryer to 200°C or oven to 230°C on fan forced setting
        2. Wash and scrub Agria potatoes (leave skin on for extra texture and nutrients)
        3. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise, then cut each half into 3-4 wedges
        4. Place wedges in a bowl of cold water for 5 minutes to remove excess starch
        5. Drain and pat dry thoroughly with paper towels
        6. In a large bowl, mix avocado oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and mixed herbs

??? note "Cooking Instructions"

    **Cook:** 30 minutes
    1. Toss dried potato wedges in the prepared seasoning mixture until evenly coated
    2. Arrange wedges on baking tray in a single layer, cook at 220°C for 25-30 minutes on a frying rack
    4. Wedges are done when golden brown and crispy outside, fluffy inside

    ??? info "Enhancements"
        - Texture Enhancement: For extra crispy wedges, parboil the potatoes for 5 minutes before seasoning and baking
        - Flavor Enhancement: Add 1 tbsp of finely grated parmesan to the seasoning mix for the last 5 minutes of cooking
        - Dipping Sauce: Serve with a side of Greek yogurt mixed with a pinch of smoked paprika and minced garlic

    ??? info "Note"
        I also added ½ tsp of cayenne and used canola oil. It took ~25 minutes in the oven and tasted great with great texture.
