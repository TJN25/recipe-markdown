
# Banh Mi (2 servings)

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 2 Vietnamese/French baguettes
    - 1 duck breast (~300g)
    - 1 medium daikon radish
    - 2 medium carrots
    - 1 cucumber
    - 2-3 bird eye chilies
    - Fresh cilantro
    - 2 spring onions
    - 4 tbsp mayonnaise
    - 1 tbsp soy sauce
    - 1-2 tsp Tabasco
    - 1/2 tsp honey (optional)

    ??? info "Chicken option"
        1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
        3 cloves garlic, grated or minced
        2 tablespoons EACH: fish sauce AND low sodium soy sauce
        1 tablespoon EACH: granulated sugar AND mayo (plus more for spreading)
        ½ teaspoon lime zest
        1-2 teaspoons sriracha (optional)

    ??? info "Quick Pickle"
        - Julienne daikon and carrots
        - Combine: 1 cup vinegar, 1 cup water, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp salt
        - Heat until sugar dissolves, cool slightly
        - Pour over vegetables
        - Pickle minimum 30 minutes

    ??? info "Pickle 2 options"
        Pickled vegetables:
        1/2 cup EACH: water, rice vinegar, AND granulated sugar
        2 teaspoons salt
        6 ounces daikon, thinly sliced (about 1/4 inch thick)
        6 ounces carrots, thinly sliced (about 1/4 inch thick)
        2 Persian cucumbers cut into circles or spears

??? note "Preparation"
    1. Score duck skin, season with salt and pepper
    2. Slice cucumber into spears
    3. Thinly slice chilies and spring onions
    4. Mix mayo, soy sauce, Tabasco, honey
    5. Heat cast iron pan medium heat
    6. Cook duck skin-side down 6-8 minutes
    7. Flip, cook 4-5 minutes more
    8. Rest 5-10 minutes, then slice

    ??? info "Assembly"
        1. Toast split baguettes (brush with duck fat if desired)
        2. Spread sauce mixture
        3. Layer duck slices
        4. Add pickled vegetables
        5. Add cucumber
        6. Top with chilies, cilantro, spring onions

    ??? info "Temperature"
        - Duck: 63°C for medium-rare
