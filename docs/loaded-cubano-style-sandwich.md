
# Loaded Cuban-Inspired Sandwich 

1. 
   - Ham, in the fry pan for a little bit
   - Swiss Cheese
   - [Guijillo Braised beef](slow-cooker-beef.md), heated 
   - [Bbq sauce](bbq-sauce.md)
   - Gherkins, sliced length ways
   - [Kewpie mayo](kewpie-style-mayo.md) or American mustard 
   - [Pickled red onions](pickled-red-onion.md)
   - Butter 
   1. Wipe with butter
   2. Press and grill like a Cubano.
