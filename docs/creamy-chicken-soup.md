
# Creamy Chicken Soup

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 2 medium carrots (about 150g), diced *(sweetness, texture)*
    - 1 large onion (about 200g), diced *(savory foundation)*
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced *(aromatic depth)*
    - 500g chicken breast, diced into 2cm pieces *(protein, substance)*
    - 300g potatoes, chopped into bite-sized pieces *(starch, heartiness)*
    - ½ tsp garlic powder *(concentrated garlic flavor)*
    - 1 tsp onion powder *(umami depth)*
    - ½ tsp paprika *(mild warmth, color)*
    - 1 tsp celery seeds *(concentrated celery flavor, aromatic)*
    - ½ tsp salt *(flavor enhancement)*
    - Fresh cracked pepper *(heat, aroma)*
    - 1 bay leaf *(herbal complexity)*
    - ½ tsp dried thyme *(earthy herb notes)*
    - ½ tsp dried oregano *(Mediterranean warmth)*
    - ¼ tsp cayenne pepper *(gentle heat)*
    - 1 litre chicken stock *(savory liquid foundation)*
    - 180ml cream *(richness, silky texture)*
    - 60g cream cheese *(tangy creaminess, body)*
    - 1½ tbsp cornflour *(thickening agent)*
    - 2 tbsp fresh parsley *(fresh herb finish, color)*

    ??? info "Chicken Seasoning (for pre-seasoning)"

        - 1 tsp salt *(tenderizing, flavor penetration)*
        - ½ tsp garlic powder *(flavor infusion)*
        - ½ tsp paprika *(color, mild warmth)*
        - ¼ tsp black pepper *(heat, aroma)*

??? note "Preparation"

    1. Dice chicken breast into 2cm pieces
    2. In a medium bowl, toss diced chicken with chicken seasoning ingredients
    3. Let chicken sit for 10-15 minutes to absorb seasoning *(salt draws out moisture then reabsorbs for tenderness)*
    4. Dice carrots and onion into 1cm pieces
    5. Mince garlic
    6. Chop potatoes into 2cm pieces (leave skin on for texture and nutrients)
    7. Lightly crush celery seeds using pestle and mortar

??? note "Method"

    1. Add seasoned chicken pieces, vegetables, potatoes, and all soup seasonings (including crushed celery seeds) to slow cooker
    2. Pour chicken stock over ingredients
    3. Cook on HIGH for 2½ hours or LOW for 5 hours 

    ??? info "Finishing Stage"
        1. In a small bowl, mix cornflour with 2 tbsp water to create slurry
        2. Stir in cream, cream cheese, and cornflour slurry
        3. Cook on HIGH for additional 30 minutes
        4. Remove bay leaf, stir in fresh parsley
        5. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper
