# KFC Coleslaw

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Vegetables"
        - 4 cups finely chopped cabbage (about 1/2 head) (base)
        - 2 tablespoons shredded carrot (color/sweetness)
        - 1 tablespoon minced onion (sharp flavor)

    ??? info "Dressing"
        - 1/4 cup mayonnaise (creamy base)
        - 2 tablespoons buttermilk (tang)
        - 2 tablespoons milk (consistency)
        - 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (sweetness)
        - 1 tablespoon lemon juice (brightness)
        - 1 tablespoon white vinegar (acidity)
        - 1/4 teaspoon salt (seasoning)
        - Pinch of pepper (spice)

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Prep"
        1. Chop cabbage very finely (rice-sized pieces)
        2. Finely grate carrot
        3. Mince onion finely

    ??? info "Dressing"
        1. In medium bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients until smooth

    ??? info "Assembly"
        1. In separate bowl, combine vegetables
        2. Pour dressing over vegetables
        3. Mix thoroughly
        4. Cover and refrigerate minimum 1 hour (longer if possible)
        5. Stir before serving
