# Fusion Taco Noodles with Red Sauce (Updated)

??? note "Ingredients"

    1-2 servings
    Base Sauce
    - 2 tbsp soy sauce (umami base)
    - 1.5-2 tsp brown sugar (balance, sweetness)
    - 2 tsp white vinegar (clean acid)
    - 1 tsp lemon or lime juice (brightness, fresh acidity)
    - ¼ chicken stock cube in 2 tbsp very hot water (depth)
    - 2-3 tbsp of your red taco sauce (replaces peanut butter and chilis)
    - ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional, for depth)
    Aromatics
    - 2-3 tsp minced garlic (base flavor)
    - 1 tsp ginger paste (warmth, complexity)
    - ½ onion, finely diced (sweetness)
    - 2 spring onions, whites and greens separated (layered flavor)
    Core Ingredients
    - Egg noodles (1-2 servings)
    - 1 egg
    - 2 tbsp oil for cooking (avocado or canola)
    Prep Stage
    1. Sauce Prep
    - Dissolve stock cube in very hot water
    - Mix in soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, and lemon/lime juice
    - Add red taco sauce and stir until well combined
    - Taste for balance - adjust sweetness or acidity as needed
    - Keep warm if possible
    2. Aromatics Prep
    - Dice onion
    - Slice spring onions (separate whites/greens)
    - Measure out minced garlic and ginger paste
    3. Noodle Prep
    - Cook until just underdone
    - Reserve ¼ cup cooking water
    - Drain, toss with tiny bit of oil
    Cooking Stage (Cast Iron Pan)
    1. Heat pan over medium-high until hot
    2. Add oil, wait until shimmering
    3. Add garlic paste, ginger paste, onion, spring onion whites
    4. Cook until onions are translucent and edges start to brown (3-4 mins)
    - Watch for light golden color
    - Pull pan off heat if cooking too fast
    - Garlic should be fragrant but not dark
    5. Push ingredients to side, add egg
    - Break up immediately when whites start turning opaque (about 30 seconds)
    - Keep pieces small
    - Mix with aromatics
    6. Add noodles and sauce
    - Keep heat medium-high
    - Toss continuously
    - Sauce should coat evenly
    Final Adjustments

    ??? info "Taste and assess"
        - If lacking brightness: add another squeeze of lemon/lime juice
        - If lacking depth: add ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
        - If too spicy: add a bit more sugar or a splash of Greek yogurt
        - If sauce separating: remove from heat, stir vigorously
        - If too thick: add reserved noodle water gradually
        - If too dry: combine 1 tbsp noodle water with:
        - ½ tbsp soy sauce
        - Tiny pinch sugar
        Finish
        - Turn off heat
        - Garnish with spring onion greens and a small wedge of lemon/lime
        - Serve immediately
