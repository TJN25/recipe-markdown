**Mexican Chipotle Chicken**

**Ingredients**
• **1 kg chicken thighs or breasts** (protein) - thighs cut into 3 pieces each, breasts sliced against the grain into 3-5 cm strips
• **100g can chipotles in adobo**, diced/minced (smoky heat, umami, acidity)
• **1 tbsp kosher salt** (seasoning, moisture retention)
• **1 tbsp smoked paprika** (smoky flavor, color)
• **1 tbsp cumin** (earthy warmth)
• **2 tsp chilli powder** (heat, depth)
• **1-2 tsp minced garlic** or 2-3 cloves, minced (aromatic, savory)
• **Lime or lemon juice** (brightness, acidity) - for serving

**Doner Kebab (4 Servings)**

# **Spiced Couscous**

**Ingredients**
- 1/2 cup couscous (starch base, texture)
- 200 mL  hot vegetable stock (flavor, cooking medium)
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin (warmth, earthiness)
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (sweet warmth)
- 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika (depth, subtle smokiness)
- Pinch of cayenne pepper (heat, optional)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil (richness, prevents sticking)
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (brightness, acidity)
- 2 tablespoons cashews, toasted and chopped (crunch)
- 1 tablespoon fresh mint, chopped (freshness)
- Salt and pepper to taste (seasoning)

**Red Taco Sauce**

*Makes approximately 300-350ml*

**Ingredients:**

• 1 cup hot water (for soaking)
• 1 can diced tomatoes
• 4 cloves garlic, unpeeled

**Pickled Red Onions**
**Ingredients**
• **1 red onion**, thinly sliced *(sweetness, texture, color)*
• **½ cup apple cider vinegar** *(acidity for pickling, flavor)*
• **1 tablespoon granulated sugar** *(balances acidity, adds sweetness)*
• **1½ teaspoons salt** *(enhances flavor, aids preservation)*
• **1 cup hot water** (not boiling) *(dilutes vinegar, helps dissolve sugar and salt)*
