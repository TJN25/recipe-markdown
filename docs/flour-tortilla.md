# Flour Tortilla Recipe (2 large tortillas)

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 100g bread flour (structure, gluten development)
    - 55 warm water (hydration)
    - 18 butter or canola oil (tenderness, flexibility)
    - 1g salt (flavor enhancement)

??? note "Preparation Steps"
    - Mix Dough:
    - In large metal bowl, combine flour and salt
    - If using butter: cut cold butter into small cubes and rub into flour until resembles breadcrumbs
    - Heat water until warm (not hot) to touch
    - Add water gradually, mixing until dough comes together
    - Knead & Rest:
    - Turn onto wooden board
    - Knead 5 minutes until smooth and not sticky
    - Divide into 2 equal portions
    - Roll each into smooth ball
    - Cover with bowl or wrap
    - Rest 20-30 minutes (allows gluten to relax)
    - Rolling & Cooking:
    - Preheat cast iron pan over medium heat
    - Dust board lightly with flour
    - Roll each ball very thin (almost see-through)
    - Cook each tortilla 20-30 seconds per side
    - Look for light brown spots and slight puffing
    - Stack in clean towel immediately

??? note "Storage"
    - Once cooled, store in airtight container
    - Separate layers with baking paper
    - Reheat briefly in cast iron pan or wrapped in foil in toaster oven

??? note "Enhancement Tips"
    - Add 1/4 tsp baking powder for more puff
    - Replace some water with milk for richness
    - Brush finished tortillas with avocado oil for shine
    - Rest dough in fridge overnight for better flavor
    - Use bench scraper for easier dough handling

    ??? info "Technique Notes"
        - Keep pan temperature consistent - if tortillas cook too fast, lower heat
        - Roll from center outward for even thickness
        - Rotate dough 45° between rolls for circular shape
        - Press gently with clean towel if tortilla doesn't puff
        - Don't stack hot tortillas on plastic - they'll sweat and become soggy
        - This scaled recipe will give you 4 burrito-sized tortillas, perfect for 2 days of meals. The dough can be made ahead and refrigerated overnight if needed.

??? note "Notes"
    Hard to roll out to right thickness, or get tortilla both cooked, but not crispy in oven or frying pan.
