
# Kecap & Coconut Chicken with Coconut Rice

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "The Rice"
        - ⅓ cup Basmati rice
        - 100g Liquid (25g Coconut Cream + Water to top up according to packet)
        - Pinch of Salt
        - **Fresh Coriander** (finely chopped, to finish)

    ??? info "The Roast"
        - 150g Chicken breast (large slices)
        - ½ Carrot (sliced thinly on the bias)
        - **Rub:**
          - 0.5 tsp Turmeric
          - 0.5 tsp Cumin
          - Salt & Pepper
          - Oil

    ??? info "The Sauce"
        - ½ White Onion (finely diced)
        - 3-4 Bird Eye Chillies (rehydrated, sliced)
        - Oil (for frying)
        - 1 tbsp **Kecap Manis**
        - 1 tsp **Soy Sauce**
        - 1 tsp **Apple Cider Vinegar**
        - ¼ tsp **Ginger Powder**
        - ⅛ tsp **Cinnamon Powder**
        - Pinch **MSG**
        - 100g Liquid (25g Coconut Cream + 75g Water)
        - **Fresh Lime** (to finish)

        ---

??? note "Method"
    1. **Preheat & Prep**
       - Preheat oven to **230°C**. Line a baking tray.
    2. **Start the Rice**
       - In a small pot, combine **rice**, **coconut liquid**, and **salt**.
       - Bring to a boil, cover tightly, reduce heat to **low**. Simmer for **13–15 minutes**.
       - Once cooked, turn off heat and let it steam with the lid on.
    3. **Roast (Chicken & Carrots)**
       - Coat chicken and carrots in the **rub ingredients** (Oil, Turmeric, Cumin, S&P).
       - Spread on the tray. **Roast for 12–14 minutes** (until charred).
    4. **Make the Sauce Base (Do this while chicken roasts)**
       - Heat a saucepan over **medium heat** with oil.
       - Add **onions** and **chillies**. Sauté for **20–30 minutes** until golden brown and soft.
       - Add **Ginger Powder** and **Cinnamon Powder**. Stir for 10 seconds.
       - Pour in **Coconut liquid**, **Kecap Manis**, **Soy Sauce**, **ACV**, and **MSG**.
       - Bring to a simmer.
    5. **Add Veg & Combine**
       - Add **Frozen Peas and Corn** to the sauce. Simmer for 3 minutes.
       - *Check consistency:* If it's too watery, boil hard for a minute. It should coat the back of a spoon.
       - Add the roasted chicken, carrots, and tray juices to the sauce. Toss to coat.
    6. **Finish & Serve**
       - **Sauce:** Turn off heat. Add a squeeze of **Lime Juice**. Taste and adjust.
       - **Rice:** Fluff the rice with a fork and stir in half the chopped **Coriander**.
       - **Plate:** Serve chicken over rice, garnished with remaining coriander.
