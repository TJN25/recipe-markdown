
# Bread Roll Recipe

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 280g bread flour
    - 210g warm water (75% hydration)
    - 5g active dry yeast
    - 5g sugar
    - 5g salt
    - 10g oil

??? note "Method"

    1. Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let bloom 10-15 minutes.

    2. Add flour, salt, and oil. Mix until shaggy dough forms.

    3. Rest 20 minutes.

    4. Stretch and fold every 30 minutes for 3 repetitions.

    5. Proof until doubled in size (2-3 hours depending on temperature).

    6. Divide into two portions and shape into rolls.

    7. Final proof 40-60 minutes.

    8. Score twice diagonally (try a deep score with the paring knife)

??? note "Baking"

    - Preheat oven to 215°C fan bake for 30 minutes
    - Place on tray, middle rack
    - Spray aggressively with water once
    - 215°C fan: 6 min
    - 175°C fan: 17 min
    - Rest on cooling rack 15 minutes uncovered
