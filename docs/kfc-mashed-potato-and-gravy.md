# KFC Mashed Potato and Gravy

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Mashed Potato"
        - 4-5 medium Agria potatoes (starchy base)
        - 30g butter (richness)
        - 2-3 tablespoons milk (creaminess)
        - Salt to taste (seasoning)

    ??? info "Gravy"
        - 1.5 tablespoons butter, divided (fat base)
        - 1/2 small onion, very finely chopped (flavor base)
        - 2.5 tablespoons all-purpose flour (thickener)
        - 1 1/4 cups beef stock (liquid and flavor)
        - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder (savory note)
        - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (spice)
        - Salt to taste (seasoning)

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Prep"
        1. Peel potatoes and cut into 2.5cm chunks
        2. Finely chop onion
        3. Measure remaining ingredients

    ??? info "Potatoes"
        1. Place potato chunks in medium saucepan
        2. Cover with cold water, add 1/2 teaspoon salt
        3. Bring to boil, reduce to medium, simmer 15-20 minutes until fork-tender
        4. Drain in colander
        5. Return to hot pot, add butter, let melt 1 minute
        6. Warm milk in microwave 20 seconds
        7. Mash potatoes, gradually add warm milk
        8. Season to taste

    ??? info "Gravy"
        1. Melt 1/2 tablespoon butter in small saucepan over medium heat
        2. Add onion, cook until soft (3-4 minutes)
        3. Add remaining butter, let melt
        4. Sprinkle flour over butter/onions
        5. Stir constantly 1-2 minutes until light brown
        6. Gradually whisk in beef stock
        7. Add garlic powder and pepper
        8. Simmer 3-5 minutes, whisking frequently until thickened
        9. Blend with stick blender until smooth
        10. Adjust seasoning
