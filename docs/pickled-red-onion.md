
# Pickled Red Onions

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1 red onion
    - 1/2 cup water
    - 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
    - 1 tbsp sugar
    - 1/2 tbsp kosher salt

??? note "Method"
    1. Slice onion
    2. Combine remaining ingredients in sauce pan on medium heat
    3. Bring to simmer
    4. Pour into jar/bowl containing sliced onion
