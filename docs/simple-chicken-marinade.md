
# Simple Chicken Marinade

??? note "To try out with freezing:"

    ??? info "Ingredients"
        - 1 kg chicken breast
        -  1 tbsp neutral oil
        -  1 tbsp soy sauce
        -  2 tsp honey
        -  2 tsp minced garlic
        -  1 tsp mustard powder
        -  ¾ tsp MSG
        -  ½ tsp black pepper
        -  ½ tsp marjoram
        -  2 tsp lemon juice

??? note "If not Freezing:"

    **Chicken:** 200 g chicken breast
    **Marinade time:** 30 minutes

    ??? info "Ingredients"

        * 1 tsp neutral oil
        * 1 tsp soy sauce
        * 1 tsp honey
        * 1 tsp minced garlic
        * 1/4 tsp mustard powder
        * 1/4 tsp MSG
        * 1/4 tsp black pepper
        * 1/4 tsp marjoram
        * 1 to 1 1/4 tsp lemon juice

    ??? info "Method"

        1. Mix all marinade ingredients.
        2. Coat the chicken well.
        3. Marinate for 30 minutes.
        4. Cook as wanted.
