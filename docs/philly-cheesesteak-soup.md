# Philly Cheesesteak Soup (Serves 1-2)

??? note "Ingredients"
    - Beef & Peppers:
    - Olive Oil: 1 tbsp
    - Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum): ½ medium, thinly sliced (User may need to buy)
    - Beef Steak: 250g, thinly sliced (or prime beef mince) (User will buy)
    - Soup Base:
    - Olive Oil: ½ tbsp
    - Onion: ½ medium, chopped
    - Carrot: ½ medium, chopped (User may need to buy)
    - Celery: 1 stalk, chopped (User may need to buy)
    - Garlic: 1-2 cloves, minced (or ½ - 1 tsp jarred)
    - All-Purpose Flour: 3 tbsp - Purpose: Thickener.
    - Worcestershire Sauce: 1 ½ tsp - Purpose: Umami, tangy.
    - Sriracha Sauce: ½ tsp - Purpose: Spice.
    - Salt & Pepper: To taste - Purpose: Seasoning.
    - Beef Broth: 3 ¼ cups (810ml), made from 1.5 - 1.75 beef stock cubes and hot water - Purpose: Liquid base, savoury flavour.
    - Heavy Cream: ½ cup (120ml) - Purpose: Richness, creaminess. (User may need to buy)
    - Cheese: 125g Cheddar (shredded) + 125g Everyday Cheese (shredded) - Purpose: Creaminess, salty flavour, umami.

    ??? info "Simplified Preparation & Cooking Instructions"
        1. Prep:
        * Beef & Veg: Thinly slice beef (if not mince). Thinly slice capsicum. Chop onion, carrot, celery. Mince garlic. Shred cheeses.
        * Broth: Dissolve 1.5 - 1.75 beef stock cubes in 3 ¼ cups (810ml) hot water.
        2. Cook Beef & Peppers (Large Saucepan or Cast Iron Pan, Medium-High Heat):
        * Heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Sauté capsicum (2 mins until softened).
        * Add beef; cook until browned (approx. 3 mins for sliced, longer for mince).
        * Remove beef and peppers; set aside.
        3. Build Soup Base (Same Pan, Medium Heat):
        * Heat ½ tbsp olive oil (if needed). Sauté onion, carrot, celery (3-5 mins until onion is soft).
        * Add garlic (cook 30 secs until aromatic).
        * Stir in flour (cook 1 min).
        * Stir in Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, pepper.
        * Gradually whisk in beef broth. Bring to a boil.
        * Reduce heat to low; simmer (15 mins until vegetables are tender).
        4. Finish Soup (Low Heat):
        * Remove from heat. Blend soup with a stick blender until smooth.
        * Return to low heat. Stir in cream and shredded cheeses until melted.
        * Stir in about half the cooked beef and pepper mixture.
        * Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
        5. Serve:
        * Ladle soup into bowls. Top with remaining beef and pepper mixture.
        Suggestions for Enhancement (Optional):
        - Deeper Vegetable Flavour: Sauté mirepoix (onion, carrot, celery) for 10-15 mins on low-medium for caramelization.
        - Deglaze: After searing beef, add a splash of broth to the hot pan, scrape bottom bits, and add to soup.
        - Smoked Paprika: Add ¼ - ½ tsp smoked paprika with the flour for smoky depth.
        - Mushrooms: Sauté sliced mushrooms (if available) with the capsicum and beef.

    ??? info "Notes on Techniques, Ingredients, and Plating"
        - Searing: Ensure pan is hot for good browning on the steak.
        - Consistency: For thicker soup, add a cornflour slurry (1 tbsp cornflour + 2 tbsp cold water) in the last 5 mins of simmering (before cream/cheese). For thinner, add more broth.
        - Leftovers: Refrigerate in an airtight container (3-4 days). Reheat gently on stovetop.
        - Plating: Ensure toppings are visible. A final crack of black pepper can enhance.
