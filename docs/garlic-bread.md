
# Simple Garlic Bread from Pane Di Casa

**Makes:** 1-2 servings (approx. 7-10 slices from a 6-8 inch loaf)

**Prep:** 10 minutes

**Cook:** 10-12 minutes

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1 small pane di casa loaf (approx. 6-8 inches long, 3-4 inches high, 4-5 inches wide)

    - 60g (approximately 4 tablespoons) butter, softened at room temperature (fat - for richness, flavour, and helps crisp the bread)
    - 2-3 cloves fresh garlic, minced (or 1 teaspoon jarred minced garlic) (flavour - the primary aromatic for garlic bread)
    - 1 teaspoon dried mixed herbs (aroma and flavour - adds complexity)
    - ¼ teaspoon salt (enhances all other flavours)
    - Optional: 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese (umami and flavour - adds a savoury, salty depth)

??? note "Equipment"
    - Toaster oven
    - Small baking tray (that fits your toaster oven)
    - Chopping board
    - Sharp knife
    - Small bowl
    - Fork or small spatula for mixing and spreading

??? note "Preparation"

    ??? info "Preheat Toaster Oven"
        1. Set your toaster oven to 180°C on the "bake" or "convection bake" setting.

    ??? info "Prepare the Bread"
        - Slice the pane di casa loaf into individual slices approximately 2cm thick.

    ??? info "Make the Garlic Butter"
        - In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic (or jarred minced garlic), dried mixed herbs, and salt.

        If using Parmesan cheese, add it now.

        Mix thoroughly with a fork or small spatula until well combined into a smooth paste.

??? note "Method"

    ??? info "Apply Garlic Butter"
        - Spread an even layer of the garlic butter mixture on one side of each bread slice.

    ??? info "Arrange on Baking Tray"
        - Place the buttered bread slices, butter-side up, in a single layer on your small baking tray.

    ??? info "Bake"
        - Place the baking tray into the preheated toaster oven.

        Bake for 8-12 minutes.

        The garlic bread is ready when the butter is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown and crispy.

        For extra crispy tops, switch the toaster oven to the "grill" setting for the last 1-2 minutes, watching carefully to prevent burning.

    ??? info "Serve"
        - Carefully remove the baking tray. Let the garlic bread cool for a minute before serving.

??? note "Notes"

    ??? info "Enhancement Suggestions"
        - **Roasted Garlic:** For a milder, sweeter garlic flavour, roast garlic cloves before mincing and adding to the butter.

        **Herb Variations:** Substitute mixed herbs with 1 tablespoon fresh chopped basil, or ¾ teaspoon dried thyme or oregano.

        **Spice:** Add a tiny pinch (less than ⅛ teaspoon) of cayenne pepper or chipotle powder to the butter for subtle warmth.

        **Acidity:** A very small squeeze of lemon juice (about ¼ teaspoon) into the butter can add brightness.

    ??? info "Plating Tips"
        - Serve warm.

        Arrange slices slightly overlapping on a plate.

        Garnish with a tiny sprinkle of fresh chopped herbs (if available) just before serving for colour and freshness.
