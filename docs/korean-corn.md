# Korean Corn Cheese

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Corn Cheese"
        - 2 cups frozen corn
        - 2 tbsp mayonnaise
        - 2 cloves garlic, minced
        - 1/2 tsp sugar
        - 1/4 tsp fine salt
        - 1/8 tsp ground white pepper
        - 1/4 tsp cayenne, or to taste
        - 1 tbsp unsalted butter
        - 1 to 1 1/4 cups shredded colby
        - 1 scallion, thinly sliced, optional

??? note "Method"
    1. Mix the corn, mayonnaise, garlic, sugar, salt, white pepper, and cayenne.
    2. Melt the butter in a small stainless saucepan or other oven-safe pan. Add the corn mixture and cook over medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring.
    3. Top with the colby.
