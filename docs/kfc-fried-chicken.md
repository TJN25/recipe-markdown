# KFC Fried Chicken

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Chicken"
        - 400-500g chicken tenderloins or boneless thighs (protein)
        - 1 cup buttermilk (tenderizer)
        - 1/2 teaspoon salt (seasoning)

    ??? info "Coating"
        - 1 cup all-purpose flour (base)
        - 1 teaspoon salt (seasoning)
        - 1/4 tablespoon thyme (herbal)
        - 1/4 tablespoon basil (aromatic)
        - 1/4 tablespoon oregano (earthy)
        - 1/2 teaspoon salt + 1/4 teaspoon ground celery seeds (celery salt substitute)
        - 1/2 tablespoon black pepper (spice)
        - 1/2 tablespoon dried mustard (pungent)
        - 2 tablespoons paprika (color/flavor)
        - 1 teaspoon minced garlic + 1/2 teaspoon salt (garlic salt substitute)
        - 1/2 tablespoon ground ginger (warmth)
        - 1.5 tablespoons white pepper (heat)
        - 1/2 teaspoon MSG (optional umami)

    ??? info "For Frying"
        - Canola/vegetable oil for shallow frying (2-3cm depth in wok)

??? note "Method"
    ??? info "Marinade (1-2 hours minimum)"

        1. Mix buttermilk and salt in medium bowl
        2. Add chicken pieces, ensure fully covered
        3. Refrigerate 1-2 hours minimum
        4. Remove from fridge 30 minutes before cooking

    ??? info "Coating Preparation"
        1. Mix all coating ingredients in large bowl
        2. Set up dredging station:
        - Bowl 1: Marinated chicken
        - Bowl 2: Seasoned flour
        - Plate: For coated chicken

    ??? info "Cooking"
        1. Heat oil in wok to 170-175°C
        2. Remove chicken from buttermilk, let excess drip
        3. Dredge thoroughly in flour mixture
        4. Optional: Double dip (buttermilk then flour again)
        5. Rest coated chicken 15 minutes
        6. Fry in batches (2-3 pieces at a time):
        - Cook in center of wok 2-3 minutes
        - Move up sides to finish
        - Turn every 2-3 minutes
        - Total time: 8-10 minutes for thighs, 6-8 for tenderloins
        - Internal temperature should reach 75°C
        7. Drain on paper towels

??? note "Notes"

    ??? info "Equipment Needed"
        - Wok for frying
        - Tongs
        - Food thermometer
        - Stick blender
        - Medium saucepan
        - Small saucepan
        - Mixing bowls
        - Sharp knife
        - Chopping boards
        - Measuring spoons/cups
        - Potato masher
        - Paper towels

    ??? info "Tips"
        - Keep oil temperature steady
        - Have lid ready for oil splatter
        - Turn chicken frequently when frying
        - Rest chicken pieces before frying
        - Blend gravy while hot
        - Check chicken internal temperature
        - Stir coleslaw before serving
