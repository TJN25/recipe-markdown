# Double-Fried Chips (French Fries)

**Serves:** 2-3 as a side

??? note "Equipment"
    - Medium stainless steel saucepan
    - Spider strainer or slotted spoon
    - Thermometer
    - Paper towels
    - Large bowl for soaking
    - Baking tray (if freezing)

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 4-5 medium Agria potatoes
    - Duck fat and/or canola oil (enough to fill pan 1/3 full)
    - Salt to taste

    ??? info "Oil Options"
        1. All duck fat (luxury version)
        2. Mixed (25-50% duck fat + canola oil)
        3. All canola oil (still good, less rich flavor)

??? note "Method"
    1. Prep:
    - Peel potatoes
    - Cut into 1cm batons (try to keep size consistent)
    - Place in bowl of cold water
    - Soak 30 minutes
    - Drain and dry thoroughly with paper towels
    - Air dry 10 minutes
    2. First Fry (130°C):
    - Fill saucepan 1/3 with chosen oil
    - Heat to 130°C
    - Fry in small batches (about 1 cup per batch)
    - Cook 5-7 minutes until tender but not brown
    - Remove with spider strainer
    - Drain on paper towels
    - Cool completely (minimum 30 minutes)
    3. Storage Options:
    - Use within 2 hours: leave at room temp
    - Use within 2 days: refrigerate in container
    - Use within 2 months: freeze on tray, transfer to freezer bag
    4. Second Fry (180°C):
    - Heat oil to 180°C
    - If frozen, don't thaw
    - Fry in small batches until golden (2-3 minutes for fresh, 3-4 for frozen)
    - Drain on fresh paper towels
    - Salt immediately while hot

??? note "Tips"
    - Monitor oil temperature between batches
    - Don't overcrowd (oil temperature drops too much)
    - Keep lid nearby for safety
    - Strain and save duck fat/oil mixture after cooling

    ??? info "Safety Notes"
        - Never leave hot oil unattended
        - Keep water away from hot oil
        - Have lid ready to smother any flames
        - Don't fill pan more than 1/3
        - Let oil cool completely before storing

    ??? info "Troubleshooting"
        - If fries are soggy: oil not hot enough
        - If browning too fast: oil too hot
        - If oily: didn't drain properly or oil too cool
        - If not crispy: needed longer first fry

??? note "Storage"
    - Cooked fries: best eaten immediately
    - Strained oil: cool completely, store in fridge
    - Par-fried potatoes: freeze or refrigerate as noted above
