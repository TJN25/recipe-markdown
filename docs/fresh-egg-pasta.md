# Pasta: Fresh Egg Pasta (Full Recipe)

    ??? info "Base Ingredients"
        - 180 bread flour (King Arthur style, high protein)
        - 5g of salt
        - 111 wet ingredients total, comprising:
        - 1 large eggs
        - 2 large yolks
        - Egg white to reach 111g

    ??? info "Equipment Needed"
        - Large wooden board
        - Sharp chef's knife
        - Kitchen scale (essential)
        - Rolling pin
        - Clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap
        - Parchment paper (for storage)

??? note "Method"
    1. Measure Ingredients (Precise):
    - Weigh flour and salt
    - Crack eggs and yolks into bowl
    - Weigh eggs/yolks
    - Add water to reach exactly 185g total wet ingredients
    2. Make Dough:
    - Mix flour and salt
    - Make well in center
    - Pour wet ingredients into well
    - Gradually incorporate flour with fork
    - Once shaggy, begin hand kneading
    3. Knead (Critical Stage):
    - Knead 8-10 minutes (set timer)
    - Use small folding motions (not bread-style)
    - Fold small amount toward you
    - Press and rotate slightly
    - Keep fingertips up when pressing
    - Dough should become silky smooth
    - Bottom should not look rough or torn
    4. Rest Dough:
    - Dust dough with flour
    - Wrap tightly in plastic
    - Rest 30 minutes minimum at room temp
    - Can rest up to 5 hours same day
    5. Rolling (By Hand):
    - Divide dough into 4 pieces (try fewer to get very large sheets)
    - Keep unused portions wrapped
    - Flatten piece to pancake thickness
    - Roll from center outward
    - Rotate 90° frequently
    - Be very liberal with flour (Very important to prevent folding over on itself!)
    - Roll until paper-thin (should see hand through)
    - Final thickness ~1mm (can stop when starts to curl at edges. It tears after that).
    6. Drying Stage:
    - Let sheets dry until leathery (15-30 mins)
    - Should be pliable but not sticky
    - Flip once to dry both sides
    - Test by touching - should feel like leather (not sure what this meant)
    7. Cutting:
    - Ensure sheets are properly dried
    - Dust generously with flour
    - Roll sheet from both ends toward center
    - Cut with sharp knife into 5mm strips
    - Use dull side of knife to lift and unroll (a skill to learn)
    - Keep strands separated

    ??? info "Storage Options"
        1. For Same Day:
        - Keep cut pasta well-floured
        - Form loose nests
        - Cover with clean towel
        - Cook within hours
        2. For Next Day (Sheets):
        - Stack slightly dry sheets
        - Place parchment between layers
        - Dust with flour
        - Wrap tightly in plastic
        - Refrigerate
        - Cut just before cooking
        3. For Next Day (Cut):
        - Dust very generously with flour
        - Form into nests
        - Store in airtight container
        - Refrigerate
        - Use within 24 hours

??? note "Cooking Instructions"
    1. Water Preparation:
    - Bring 1.5L water to boil
    - Add salt when almost boiling (15g of salt)
    - Return to full boil uncovered
    2. Cooking:
    - Add pasta to rapidly boiling water
    - Stir immediately
    - Cook 1-2 minutes
    - Taste at 1 minute
    - Remove with tongs/spider (don't drain)
    - Add directly to hot sauce

    ??? info "Key Tips"
        - Never drain in colander
        - Reserve pasta water before removing pasta
        - Sauce should be ready before cooking pasta
        - Fresh pasta needs very salty water
        - Cut pasta just before cooking if possible
        - Always test for doneness - timing varies
