# Assassin's Pasta

**Serves:** 1

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 90g spaghetti (one portion)
    - 90ml passata (sauce base)
    - 1 tbsp tomato paste (concentrated flavor)
    - 1-2 cloves garlic, minced (flavor base)
    - 1-2 dried bird's eye chilies (heat to taste)
    - 2 tbsp olive oil (for proper coverage)
    - Salt (seasoning)
    - 125ml hot vegetable stock (start with 60ml)

??? note "Preparation"
    1. Crush 1 bird's eye chili in pestle and mortar
    2. Keep 1 whole for oil infusion
    3. Mix passata, tomato paste, and 60ml hot stock
    4. Mince garlic
    5. Have remaining stock hot and ready

    ??? info "Cast Iron Preparation"
        1. Heat pan over medium heat for 5 minutes
        2. Test with water droplet - should bead and dance
        3. Pan should be hot but not smoking

    ??? info "Cooking Method"
        1. Add olive oil to heated pan
        2. Add whole chili and garlic
        3. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant
        4. Remove whole chili if desired
        5. Arrange dry spaghetti in sunburst pattern
        6. Sprinkle crushed chili evenly
        7. Pour passata mixture carefully around pasta
        8. Maintain medium-high heat
        9. Add remaining stock in 2-3 additions as needed
        10. Minimal stirring - just enough to prevent burning
        11. Cook 10-12 minutes until al dente with crispy sections

    ??? info "Visual Cues"
        - Oil should shimmer, not smoke
        - Sauce should actively bubble
        - Edges of pasta will brown
        - Listen for sizzling to decrease before adding more liquid

    ??? info "Finishing"
        - Season with salt to taste
        - Optional: small handful of grated parmesan
        - Fresh basil leaves if desired

??? note "Technical Notes"
    - Single portion needs closer attention (less liquid buffer)
    - May cook slightly faster than larger portion
    - Watch edges carefully as less pasta means more exposed pan surface
    - Use wooden spoon to test pasta doneness

    ??? info "Plating"
        - Warm pasta bowl
        - Arrange crispy sections up
        - Fresh basil garnish
        - Drizzle of extra virgin olive oil
        - Optional parmesan
        - Whole chili for garnish if you kept it

    ??? info "Heat Guide"
        - Mild: 1/2 crushed chili, no whole chili
        - Medium: 1 crushed chili, infuse with whole
        - Hot: 1 crushed + keep whole chili in
        - Extra Hot: 2 crushed chilies + whole chili
