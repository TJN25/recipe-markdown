# Fettuccine with Mushroom White Wine Sauce

    ??? info "Ingredients for Pasta"
        - Fettuccine (home made, very wide cut
        - 100g mushrooms, sliced
        - 1/2 onion, finely diced
        - 1 celery stalk, finely diced
        - 1 carrot, finely diced
        - 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
        - 1/2 cup white wine
        - 1 cup fresh chicken stock (home made)
        - 1/2 cup parmesan, grated (parmigiano reggiano)
        - 3-4 tbsp butter
        - Olive oil
        - Salt

??? note "Method"
    1. Prep all ingredients first:
    - Dice mirepoix
    - Dice mushrooms
    - Mince garlic
    - Grate parmesan
    2. Cook:
    - Heat cast iron frypan: 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil
    - Sauté mirepoix 5-7 minutes
    - Add mushrooms, cook until golden (dark for portobello) (5-6 mins)
    - Add garlic, cook 1 minute (taste and salt at this point)
    - Add wine, reduce by half
    - Add stock, simmer to reduce
    - Cook pasta in large saucepan
    - Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water
    - Combine pasta with sauce
    - Add parmesan, butter
    - Adjust with pasta water
    - Season

    ??? info "Timing Tips"
        1. Start pasta water first
        2. Make sauce while water heats
        3. Prep salad while sauce reduces
        4. Dress salad just before serving pasta

    ??? info "Plating"
        1. Pasta:
        - Twist pasta with tongs
        - Add extra sauce
        - Garnish with parsley, parmesan, pepper

    ??? info "Remember"
        - Reserve pasta water
        - Taste and adjust seasoning
        - Serve immediately while pasta is hot
