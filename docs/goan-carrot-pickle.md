
# Carrot Pickle

**Yield:** about 300 g

??? note "Ingredients"

    * 250 g carrots
    * 60 mL rapeseed oil
    * 1 1/2 tsp sugar
    * salt, to taste

    ??? info "For the paste"

        * 90 mL white vinegar
        * 4 dried Kashmiri chillies
        * 4 fresh long green chillies
        * 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
        * 1/8 tsp fenugreek seeds
        * 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
        * a small piece of ginger, about 1/4 inch
        * 5 black peppercorns
        * 1/4 tsp turmeric powder

    ??? info "For frying"

        * 7 to 9 garlic cloves, minced

??? note "Method"

    1. Peel the carrots, trim the ends, and cut into thick matchsticks. Salt them lightly.
    2. Grind the vinegar, dried chillies, fresh chillies, mustard seeds, fenugreek, cumin, garlic, ginger, peppercorns, and turmeric to a fine paste.
    3. Slice the extra garlic.
    4. Heat the rapeseed oil in a pan and fry the garlic for a couple of minutes until aromatic.
    5. Add the ground paste and fry until the oil starts to rise.
    6. Add the carrots, sugar, and salt as needed.
    7. Cook on low until the carrots are tender-crisp.
    8. Cool, jar, and leave to mellow before using.
