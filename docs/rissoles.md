# Rissoles

**Serves:** 2–3

??? note "Ingredients"

    ??? info "Rissoles"
        - 3/4 small brown onion
        - 6 tbsp panko breadcrumbs (or normal)
        - 375 g beef mince (ground beef)
        - 3/4 small carrot
        - 3/4 egg
        - 3/4 large garlic clove, minced
        - 3/8 tsp dried thyme
        - 3/8 tsp oregano
        - 3/8 tsp each salt and pepper
        - 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

??? note "Cooking"
    - 1 tbsp oil

    ??? info "Serving"
        - Tomato ketchup

??? note "Method"
    1. Grate the onion into a large bowl. Add panko, mix briefly to coat in the onion juices.
    2. Grate carrot into the bowl. Add remaining Rissoles ingredients. Mix well. Scoop up about 3 tbsp of mixture, form a 1.7 cm / 2/3" thick pattie. Indent the middle slightly. Repeat with remaining mixture.
    3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add half the patties and cook for 4 minutes or until dark golden. Press LIGHTLY with spatula, if you press hard, you’ll press the juices out.
    4. Flip and cook the other side for 3–4 minutes until golden.
    5. Transfer to plate, repeat with remaining rissoles.
    6. Serve with tomato ketchup.
