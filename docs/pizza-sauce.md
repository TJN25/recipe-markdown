
# Quick Pizza Sauce

??? note "Ingredients"
    - 1 can diced tomatoes, drained (reserve liquid)
    - 2 tbsp tomato paste (purpose: thickening, intense tomato flavor)
    - 1 clove garlic, minced (purpose: aromatic base)
    - 1 tsp dried oregano (purpose: classic pizza flavor)
    - ¼ tsp salt (purpose: enhance flavors)
    - Pinch of sugar (purpose: balance acidity)
    - Optional: pinch of red pepper flakes for heat
    Blend with stick blender until smooth. Should be thick - add reserved liquid only if needed.
