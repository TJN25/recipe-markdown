# Beef and Feta Orzo

??? note "Ingredients"

    - 1.5 tbsp olive oil
    - 125g beef mince
    - 0.5 brown onion
    - 1.5 cloves garlic
    - 1 tsp smoked paprika
    - 0.5 tsp ground cinnamon
    - 0.5 tsp dried marjoram
    - 2 tbsp tomato paste
    - 80-90ml chicken stock
    - 1-2 tsp white wine vinegar
    - 100g orzo
    - 0.5 chicken stock cube
    - 7-8g fresh dill
    - 0.5-1 tsp sumac
    - 50g feta

??? note "Method"

    1. Dice onion, slice garlic.

    2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in large frying pan over medium-high. Season and crumble in beef mince, fry 6-8 minutes until golden and crisp.

    3. Add onion, garlic, paprika, cinnamon, salt. Cook 4-5 minutes until soft.

    4. Add marjoram and tomato paste, cook 2-3 minutes until dark and sticky.

    5. Add stock and vinegar, reduce by ⅓, stirring and scraping pan.

    6. Add orzo, stock cube, 250ml boiling water. Mix and bring to boil.

    7. Reduce to medium, simmer 8-10 minutes until orzo is soft and sauce is glossy. Add water if needed.

    8. Roughly chop dill, mix with crumbled feta, sumac, and ½ tbsp oil.

    9. Divide orzo between bowls, top with feta mixture.
